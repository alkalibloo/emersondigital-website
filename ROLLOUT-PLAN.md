### Pre-Launch Checklist (Per App)

```

‚ñ° Press kit ready (logo, screenshots, description)
‚ñ° Product Hunt draft prepared
‚ñ° Beta tester list of 20+ people
‚ñ° Launch day content queued
```

### Launch Day Playbook

**Morning**:
1. Push Product Hunt live (12:01 AM PT ideal)
2. Tweet announcement with link
3. Post on LinkedIn
4. Email newsletter subscribers
5. Post in relevant Discord/Slack communities

**Throughout Day**:
6. Respond to ALL comments/questions
7. Share on Reddit (relevant subreddits)
8. Instagram/TikTok story behind-the-scenes
9. Thank early supporters publicly

**Post-Launch**:
10. Write "launch retrospective" blog post
11. Collect testimonials
12. Plan v1.1 based on feedback
