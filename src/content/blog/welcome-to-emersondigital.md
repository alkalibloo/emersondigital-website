Welcome to the official EmersonDigital blog! We're excited to share our journey with you as we build software that makes a real difference in people's lives.

## Who We Are

EmersonDigital is a small software studio with a big mission: create thoughtful software for work and play. We believe that great software should be both powerful and delightful, solving real problems while being a joy to use.

## Our Apps

We're currently working on three exciting projects:

### Emerson's Learning Games

A collection of educational games designed specifically for young children. Our goal is to make learning fun, engaging, and screen-time parents can feel good about.

### BIMGeoSpace

A professional tool that bridges Building Information Modeling (BIM) with geospatial data. Perfect for architects, engineers, and urban planners who need to see their projects in real-world context.

### Neon Task Grid

A visually stunning task management app with a unique grid-based interface. Because productivity tools should inspire you to get things done, not bore you.
